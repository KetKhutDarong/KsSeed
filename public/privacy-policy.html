Introduction

Welcome to KSSEED ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting the personal information of every visitor and user of our website at ksseedcambodia.pages.dev and any related services we operate.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Our Commitment: KSSEED will never sell your personal information to third parties. We collect only what is necessary to serve you better and improve our agricultural resources for Cambodian farmers.
2

Information We Collect

We collect information in a few different ways depending on how you interact with our website:

Personal Information

Information you provide directly to us, such as when you fill out a contact form or submit a farmer story:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Province or location in Cambodia
  • Any message or story content you submit

Usage & Activity Data

Automatically collected as you browse our website:

  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • Search queries entered on our website
  • Links and buttons you click
  • Referral source (how you found our website)

Device & Browser Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • IP address (anonymized where possible)
3

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect only for legitimate purposes that help us serve farmers and visitors better:

  • Respond to your contact requests, questions, and inquiries promptly
  • Provide personalized seed and crop recommendations based on your region
  • Improve our website content, navigation, and overall user experience
  • Analyze website performance to identify and fix technical issues
  • Send updates about KSSEED products and research (only if you opted in)
  • Publish farmer success stories with your explicit written consent
  • Comply with legal obligations under Cambodian law
Note: We will never use your personal information for purposes beyond what is described here without notifying you and obtaining your consent first.

Data Protection

KSSEED takes the security of your personal information seriously. We implement reasonable and appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data from:

  • Unauthorized access or disclosure
  • Accidental loss or destruction
  • Unlawful processing or misuse

Our website is hosted on a secure platform (Cloudflare Pages) with HTTPS encryption. Access to user data is restricted to authorised KSSEED team members only.

Important: While we take strong precautions, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to avoid sharing sensitive personal information unnecessarily.
