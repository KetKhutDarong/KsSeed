🌿 Overview

Climate Resilience
Through Research

Cambodia faces increasing challenges from climate change, including prolonged dry seasons, unpredictable rainfall patterns, and extreme heat events that threaten millions of smallholder farmers.

KSSEED responds by developing vegetable varieties specifically adapted to these harsh conditions — identifying and breeding natural drought tolerance and heat resistance traits so farmers can maintain productive harvests no matter the season.

🧬 Methodology

Our Adaptation Strategy

A rigorous four-stage process from wild germplasm collection to farmer-ready climate-resilient varieties.

1
🌍

Germplasm Collection

We collect diverse seed varieties from across Cambodia and the world, including wild relatives and traditional landraces with proven natural climate resilience traits developed over centuries.

2
🔬

Trait Identification

Using advanced genomic screening and phenotyping, we identify and map drought tolerance, heat resistance, and water-use efficiency traits at the molecular level to accelerate breeding.

3
🌱

Breeding Program

Through controlled cross-breeding and multi-generation selection, we develop new varieties combining climate resilience, high yield potential, great taste, and farmer-preferred traits.

4
🌾

Field Trials

We conduct multi-season field trials across Cambodia's agro-climatic zones to validate performance under real farm conditions — extreme heat, floods, and water scarcity.

⚠️ Climate Threats

Addressing Climate Challenges

Our seeds are engineered to thrive through Cambodia's four most severe climate stressors.

🌡️

Heat Tolerance

Our varieties maintain full productivity when daytime temperatures exceed 35°C, protecting farmer income during Cambodia's hottest months and increasingly frequent heat waves.

💧

Drought Resistance

Deep-rooted varieties with superior water-use efficiency thrive during extended dry spells, cutting irrigation needs and lowering production costs for smallholder farmers.

🌧️

Flood Tolerance

Specially bred varieties tolerate waterlogged soil for extended periods, ideal for low-lying paddies and flood-prone areas throughout Cambodia's heavy monsoon season.

🌪️

Storm Resilience

Strong, compact plant architecture and robust stems help our varieties withstand heavy winds and driving rains from tropical storms and monsoon systems.
