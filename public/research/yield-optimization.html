Increasing Farmer Profitability Through Better Genetics

KSSEED's yield optimization program develops vegetable varieties that produce significantly higher quantities of better-quality crops per plant. We combine improved genetics with rigorous agronomic testing to help Cambodian farmers increase income, feed their families, and contribute to national food security.

Our research examines every aspect of plant performance вҖ” from early germination vigor and root strength to fruit size, fruit number, marketability, and shelf life. Each variety released by KSSEED has been proven to outperform conventional local varieties under real Cambodian farming conditions.
