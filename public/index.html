About KsSEED

Welcome to KS Seed

🎯

Our Vision

Establishing an international-standard vegetable seed production station (OP & F1) in Cambodia to supply both domestic and international markets.

🌱

Our Mission

Starting with the research of traditional local parent lines to produce high-yield, climate-resilient OP and F1 vegetable seeds. Operating as an eco-business, our scope covers research, contract seed production, packaging, marketing, and providing technical support for local vegetable cultivation in Cambodia.

💎

Core Values

1. A "Eco-Business" enterprise that operates from A to Z (from vegetable seed research to the final farmer's harvest).

2. The very first Cambodian-owned company to start in the field of vegetable seed production (covering the full production cycle), creating numerous job opportunities and improving the livelihoods of many local people.

3. Researching traditional local vegetable varieties for seed production (OP) and developing new parental lines (F1).

Our Journey

2018-2019

Foundation

KsSeed was established and produced the 25a experimental Beans, pods and packaged the market trial Beans, pods and bindweed.

2020-2021

Market growth

We have expanded the production of Beans, pods, hair dispersal variety 1 hektometer, and have extended the market to 24 varieties. In addition, we have expanded the contract for bindweed.

2022-2023

Growing market

We have expanded the market to 44 varieties, produced Beans, pods of the Ratanak variety, and signed a contract to produce bindweed.

2024-2025

Currently

We have expanded the market to 74 varieties and signed a production contract with the Otdam Meanchey Union Community for mung bean and Beans, pods seeds.
